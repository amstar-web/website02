Tour name

Enjoy the adventure!

Fly over unspoiled Mayan jungle on zip lines and enjoy magnificent views, swim in underground rivers, ride an amphibious vehicle and raft through mystical underground waters. Experience Xplor the newest, hottest, coolest adventure park!

Xplor adventure park in Riviera Maya is a unique underground world with thrilling circuits that invite you to rediscover your senses and emotions within nature. Enjoy the adventure while being guided from take off to landing. Fly over the jungle on zip lines with awesome views and refreshing water landings. Later, take on a challenge crossing hanging bridges and exploring 3.11 miles of fascinating jungle pathways on amphibious vehicles.
